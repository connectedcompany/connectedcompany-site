AI positioning

Independent advice across a fragmented AI ecosystem.

Gridplane is an AI play in the sense that we help organisations make sound decisions about where AI fits, where it does not, and what architecture, controls, and operating changes are required to make it useful. Our role is not to push a preferred platform. It is to bring clarity across a crowded ecosystem and help clients move with confidence.

Independent viewpoint

Advice before allegiance.

We help leadership teams evaluate options without steering them toward a preselected vendor, hyperscaler, model provider, or tooling stack. The point is fit, not affiliation.

Complexity to clarity

AI decisions rarely sit in one place. They span cloud, data platforms, models, governance, security, integration, and operating design. We help clients make sense of how those pieces fit together.

Applied AI
